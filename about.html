Welcome to our restaurant! We are a family of six who have recently moved to Leiria from India, bringing with us a passion for authentic and delicious cuisine. Our goal is to share our love of food with the local community and offer a taste of our home country through our menu. Our recipes have been passed down through generations and are made with only the freshest ingredients. We take great pride in our cooking and aim to provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all our customers. At our restaurant, you can expect a wide range of dishes that are bursting with flavor and spice, from classic Indian curries to unique street food favorites. Whether you're a seasoned foodie or trying Indian cuisine for the first time, we have something to suit all tastes. We believe that food brings people together and we look forward to serving you and your loved ones. Come join us for an unforgettable culinary journey that will transport your taste buds straight to the streets of India.
